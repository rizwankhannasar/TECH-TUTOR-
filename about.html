About TechTutor

Empowering the next generation of tech leaders with structured learning and hands-on practice.

Our Mission

TechTutor is a comprehensive educational platform designed at MUST (Mirpur University of Science and Technology). We bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical technical skills so that every student can build a strong foundation and advance their career.

What We Offer

  • Structured courses — AI, CSS, Cyber Security, JavaScript, and more
  • Video lectures — Watch at your own pace, anytime
  • Quizzes — 10 MCQs per course to test your understanding (60% to pass)
  • Progress tracking — Enroll in courses and track your learning journey

Why Choose Us

Expert-led curriculum, project-based learning, and lifetime access to resources. Whether you are a student aiming to master Artificial Intelligence or a professional learning Web Development, TechTutor provides the tools and structure you need.
